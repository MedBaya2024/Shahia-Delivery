HOW IT WORKS

FAST FRANCHISE SET-UP

Franchise opportunities have never been so fast. With our quick training, easy menus and ready-to-go branding, you gain a plug-and-play franchise fit for purpose.

DELIVERY OF PLATFORM MANAGEMENT

We don’t just create delivery-first brands, we make sure they’re fully managed for you too. We arrange your listings on the top delivery platforms, including images, menus and prices. We consistently update your information for you and bring the best deals to the table. Meaning you can focus on the food and not the fiddly tech.

SUPPLY CHAIN

More bang for your buck. Through our trusted supply chain, you get the best deals. Once you send the inventory, we deliver the necessary ingredients straight to you at the best price.

MARKETING

We look after all the marketing, making crave-worthy campaigns that put your food centre stage. Whether it’s through scrumptious visual content or our famous foodies, each of our brands is making waves in global markets. You don’t need to worry about pushing your menus, with our dedicated marketing teams, your franchise will be firmly in the spotlight.

SIGN-UP

Franchise opportunities shouldn’t be complicated. So we made our foodie delivery brands ready to go (for both you and your customers). When you sign up with us, we do the heavy lifting. We deliver the brand, launch the operations and get you cooking fast. In just a matter of weeks, you’ll have pans firing and taste buds tingling.

SET UP

Prep is important. Our teams will train you how to manage, operate and cook each brand. We’ll provide the menus, materials and marketing to get you rolling out delicious food fast. Our leading tech equipment will centralise your orders and show your ratings, reviews and wait times. Getting the best ingredients at the right price is key. Using our supply chain you can keep your margins low and order count high.

SELL

Now it’s time to cook, we’ll focus on the selling. We put you on the top delivery platforms and manage them for you. We keep an eye on your listings and update your menus when needed, meaning you can start delivering without the heavy selling.
